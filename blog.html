Babysitting

I have been babysitting since I was 13. I have always had a strong connection with kids; I would be the person who you would find near the kid's table or who was watching over the little ones at a family event. As soon as I was of age, I reached out to members of my church and offered up my services. Then, a few years later, I joined care.com, a babysitting website where families can locate and reach out to local caregivers. It took some effort to start off, but after some time, I had found my first family.

Today, I have cared for around 14 families, with their children's ages ranging from newborn to middle school. It is truly one of my passions and I enjoy all the time I get to spend with these kids.



Some other interests that have made me who I am today are: American Sign Language, volunteering, and organizing.
