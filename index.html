Melody Lacombe

My name is Melody and I am a student and an artist.

My vision is to create art that will attract people from all walks of life, with the mission to connect us all at a deeper level. I create works of fiction and nonfiction that tackle issues within today's society, and that make audiences think critically about their day to day lives.

I am an advocate for teaching myself new skills... through one of my high school classes I was able to code this website and make the logo in the top left corner of the screen. Since the end of that class, I have continued on my own!

There so many things that make me who I am; Film, Writing, Website Design, and so much more.

I look forward to expanding that list!
