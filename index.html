Life with Melody

Filming, Writing, Loving, Living

My vision is to create art that will attract people from all walks of life, with the mission to connect
with them at a deeper level. I create works of fiction and nonfiction film that tackle issues within today's
society. More often than not I will lean towards fiction because my imagination is a huge part of me, and I find
comfort with the knowledge that there are no wrong answers in fiction. I find myself creating comedic pieces that
are relatable but will at times create some darker pieces that make the audience think about their lives.
